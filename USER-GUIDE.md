## 🏠 Getting Started

### 1️⃣ First Launch — Onboarding
When you open StudyEN for the first time:

1. 🌐 **Choose your language** — English 🇬🇧 or Tiếng Việt 🇻🇳
2. ✏️ **Enter your name** — Your adventure begins!
3. 🎉 **Welcome celebration** — Confetti & your mascot greets you!

### 2️⃣ Navigation
Use the **top navigation bar** to explore:

| Icon | Page | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| 🏠 | **Home** | Dashboard with XP, pet, world map, mystery box |
| 💬 | **Chat** | AI conversation practice with different roles |
| 📚 | **Vocab** | Flashcards & word learning |
| 🎤 | **Speaking** | Voice recording & pronunciation feedback |
| 🎮 | **Games** | Mini-games: Catch the Word & Word Matching |
| 📖 | **Story** | Interactive story adventures |
| 🎭 | **Role Play** | Real-life scenario practice |

## 🎮 Features

### 💬 AI Chat
Chat with AI in different roles:
- 👥 **Conversation Partner** — Casual everyday talk
- 💼 **Job Interviewer** — Practice for interviews
- 🎓 **Tutor** — Get corrections & tips

> **Earn:** +5 XP per message • +5 🪙 every 5 messages

### 📚 Vocabulary
- 🃏 **Flashcards** — Tap to flip & learn
- 🔍 **Search** — Find words quickly
- ➕ **Add words** — Build your own word list
- ✅ **Mark as learned** — Track your progress

> **Earn:** +10 XP per word mastered • +5 🪙

### 🎤 Speaking Practice
1. 🎙️ Press the microphone to record
2. 🗣️ Speak a sentence in English
3. 📊 Get scores for pronunciation, grammar & fluency

> **Earn:** XP = score × 3 • +15 🪙 for score ≥ 7

### 🎮 Mini-Games

#### 🎯 Catch the Word
- Words fall from the sky!
- Tap the word matching the given meaning
- ❤️ You have 3 lives — don't miss the right word!

#### 🧩 Word Matching
- Match English words with their meanings
- Tap a word, then tap its translation
- Complete all pairs to win!

### 📖 Story Mode
- Choose from interactive stories (The Magic Café ☕, The Lost Castle 🏰)
- Read dialogue from fun characters
- Make choices that affect the story
- Learn vocabulary in context

> **Earn:** XP based on your choices!

### 🎭 Role Play
Practice real-life scenarios:
- 🍕 **Restaurant** — Order food like a pro
- 🏫 **School** — Meet new classmates
- 🤝 **Meeting Friends** — Social conversations

Use suggested responses or type your own!

## ⭐ XP & Leveling

| Action | XP Earned |
|--------|-----------|
| Send a chat message | +5 XP |
| Master a vocabulary word | +10 XP |
| Speaking practice | Score × 3 XP |
| Complete a story | Varies |
| Win a mini-game | Score-based |

- 🏆 Level up formula: `100 × 1.5^(level-1)` XP per level
- 🪙 +50 coins bonus on every level up!
- 🔥 **Daily streak** — Visit every day to keep your streak!
